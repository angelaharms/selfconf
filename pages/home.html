What's this all about?

The next step after 1DevDay Detroit. We love DevDays, and with half the organizing team helping with self.conference, we wanted to create something just as affordable and accessible with twice as much amazing content. We're packing two days with mobile, web, hardware, software, process, and team talks to provide you the best opportunity to grow, mentor and learn as yourself, a part of the community, and a part of Detroit's exploding economy.
